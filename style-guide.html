Style Guide

UI guidelines & Inspiration

I tried to emulate the official government look as much as I could while making a few adjustment. I changed the header to be sticky because of UX personal preference, and adapted the appearance of the footer to the content I had created. I kept the original color palette and tried to keep the font similar. Here I used Montserrat semi bold, medium and regular. I also found SVG files of both official Canadian Government logos.
